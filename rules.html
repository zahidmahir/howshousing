Rules and Other Prizes

You can only fill out the survey once during the month that it is activated. Multiple submissions will disqualify you from a chance to win the prize.


If you filled out the survey and live in the building that has the most constructive responses, you will automatically receive one extra programming point.


The How's Housing survey is given in October, December, and March. If you fill out all three surveys you will be entered into a chance to win a $150 gift card at the end of the year, along with the chance to win whatever the price will be for March. For example, if March's grand prize is a $100 gift card, you will be entered into a chance to win that $100 gift card plus an additional $150 gift card if you filled out all three surveys constructively. Total value $250.


Just by filling out the survey constructively when it is available, you will be entered into a chance to win the prize of that month.
